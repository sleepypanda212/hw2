Announcements

Dear Metro Nails Clients,
As of 5/1/2022, We are still requiring both our clients and staff to wear mask while in the salon. Please adhere to this minimal request for all our benefits. Thank you.
We are making all necessary plans to ensure a safe and healthy environment for you and our staff, and have instituted a new Health And Safety policy towards that end.

Metro Nails Health and Safety Policy.
1. All services will be by Appointment Only until further notice.
2. All clients and staff Must wear an appropriate mask at all times while in the salon
3. All clients must wash their hands when they first enter the salon.

As of 1/1/2014, we are unable to accept Tips/Gratuities on your credit card payments. Cash Tips are still greatly appreciated.
In addition, we have instituted a price change, a first since we opened in 2008, to keep pace with current business expenses. Please refer to our Menu page for the changes.
We appreciate your understanding and hope to see you all soon.
Thank you.
